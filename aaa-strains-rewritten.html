What Are AAA Strains?

Cannabis gets graded from A to AAAA+. AAA sits in the high-quality range. Here's what makes them special:

  • Consistent quality - Every batch meets strict standards
  • Dense, well-trimmed buds with vibrant colors
  • Strong aromas that smell amazing
  • THC levels between 18-25%
  • Hand-trimmed and properly cured

AAA strains are reliable. You know what you're getting every time. The buds are dried and cured perfectly for smooth smoke and great flavor.

How to Spot Quality AAA Strains

Look for these signs when shopping online:

Visual Clues

  • Frosty appearance - Covered in white crystals (trichomes)
  • Bright colors - Vibrant greens, purples, or oranges
  • Well-preserved pistils - Orange or amber hairs

Smell Test

  • Strong, distinct aromas - Each strain smells unique
  • Fresh scent - Not musty or stale
  • Matches the description - Sweet strains smell sweet, diesel strains smell fuel-like

Texture Check

  • Dense but not compressed - Feels solid but not rock-hard
  • Slightly sticky - From natural resin
  • Breaks apart easily - Doesn't crumble to dust
